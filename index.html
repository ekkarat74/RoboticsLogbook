Who We Are
DroneAnalytics started out with one product, DroneLogbook (www.dronelogbook.com) . This was developed to support the growing commercial UAV industry. Our experience there has made us one of the premier global providers of autonomous logging solutions. DroneLogbook is used by thousands of users including global mining, energy, agricultural, media, insurance and transportation companies.
What We Do
Now, as the industry is expanding into self driving vehicles and aquatic robots we are expanding our offerings as well. Our core DroneAnalytics platform is modular and configurable allowing us to adapt it to the needs of each autonomous market segment.
The core functionality we track and provide:
  • Usage History – Via automated telemetry import
  • Maintenance History
  • Inspection Schedules
  • Personnel Tracking (Skills, Accrued Time by Role, etc)
  • Incidents
  • Fleet Management
  • Component Management
  • Notifications

    • In addition, our platform allows for notifications to be sent if events are triggered. These events are above and beyond the performance anomalies tracked by the autonomous vehicle manufacturers. They are operational notifications based on anomalies from planned missions and regulatory compliance (government and internal organizational).

    Our solutions are AWS cloud based and scalable for thousands of units or personnel. We offer it as a private label that allows for configuration and customization.
